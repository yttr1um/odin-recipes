Pancake Recipe

The recipe:

This is a bulk whole wheat pancake mix recipe that my family loves, and it is very easy to make! It also makes good waffles.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 teaspoons salt

steps

  1. Mix 1 cup of pancake mix, 1 cup of milk, 1 egg, and 2 tablespoons of oil together in a large bowl.
  2. Cook pancakes on a hot griddle and serve with your choice of toppings.
